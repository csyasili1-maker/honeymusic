Who We Are

A creative home for Indian sound and story

Honey Music is envisioned as more than a music label. It is a creative ecosystem built to discover talent, shape original ideas and bring memorable music to audiences across generations.

Rooted in a deep love for cinema, music and meaningful storytelling, the platform brings singers, composers, lyricists, musicians, technicians and visual creators into one collaborative space.

Every Honey Music project begins with respect for the artist and the audience. The goal is simple: nurture strong voices, build lasting creative careers and produce work that carries emotion, craft and cultural connection.

The Beginning

Born from a lifelong love of music and cinema

Honey Music grew from a shared belief that powerful creative work needs more than a stage. It needs patient support, clear direction, disciplined production and people willing to stand behind an artist's vision.

Founder Kandregula Naidu's journey through entrepreneurship, real estate and film production created the foundation for an ambitious new platform. Co-Founder Kandregula Bhagya Lakshmi brought operational strength, artistic sensitivity and a people-first approach to that vision.

Together, they are building Honey Music as a place where creativity can become a profession, where emerging talent can be heard, and where music and cinema can create meaningful employment and enduring cultural value.

Founders of Honey Music

Founder Portrait Coming Soon Honey Music
Founder, Honey Music

Kandregula Naidu

A distinguished entrepreneur, acclaimed real estate developer and film producer whose journey is defined by vision, perseverance and an unwavering commitment to excellence.

Based in Anakapalli, Andhra Pradesh, Kandregula Naidu has established himself as one of the region's respected names in the real estate and construction sector. Over the years, he has successfully developed multiple landmark residential and commercial projects. His extensive expertise, strategic foresight and reputation for delivering quality have earned the trust of countless clients and stakeholders.

Driven by a lifelong passion for cinema, he expanded his entrepreneurial journey into filmmaking. His debut Telugu feature film, Anakapalli, featuring a stellar ensemble cast, marked the beginning of an ambitious cinematic vision. With his second and third feature films already under production, he is steadily building a legacy as a producer dedicated to meaningful, entertaining and impactful cinema.

His long-term vision is to produce films that resonate with audiences across generations while contributing significantly to the growth of the Indian film industry.

His love for music and storytelling inspired the birth of Honey Music, envisioned not merely as a music label but as a creative ecosystem that nurtures talent, celebrates artistic excellence and provides opportunities for aspiring musicians, singers, composers, lyricists and technicians.

Through Honey Music, he seeks to empower emerging artists, create meaningful employment within the creative industry and contribute to the ever-evolving landscape of cinema and music. With an entrepreneurial spirit, a deep appreciation for the arts and a commitment to innovation, Kandregula Naidu continues to shape ventures designed to leave a lasting impact on business and the creative world.

Co-Founder Portrait Coming Soon Honey Music
Co-Founder, Honey Music

Kandregula Bhagya Lakshmi

A passionate advocate for art, music and creative expression, bringing stability, discipline and a deeply people-centric approach to Honey Music.

Kandregula Bhagya Lakshmi is a strong pillar of support behind every entrepreneurial and creative venture undertaken by Kandregula Naidu. Through her dedication, sound judgment and unwavering commitment, she has played an instrumental role in the growth of their businesses.

With extensive experience in managing business operations and organizational affairs, she brings clarity and dependable leadership to the foundation of Honey Music. Her ability to oversee operations while nurturing a collaborative work culture has been invaluable in shaping the company's vision and long-term goals.

A true lover of cinema and music, Bhagya Lakshmi has a refined appreciation for meaningful melodies, soulful compositions and quality content. Her passion for discovering and promoting exceptional musical talent is one of the driving forces behind Honey Music's mission.

She firmly believes that music has the power to inspire, connect and transcend boundaries, and she is committed to building a platform that consistently delivers memorable musical experiences to audiences.

As Co-Founder, she shares a vision of making Honey Music a home for talented singers, composers, lyricists, musicians and creators by providing opportunities that encourage innovation and artistic excellence. Together with Kandregula Naidu, she is dedicated to fostering emerging talent, creating employment within the creative industry and contributing to the continued growth of Indian cinema and music.

Her passion, integrity and unwavering support continue to inspire the Honey Music journey, helping transform a shared dream into a platform committed to producing music that resonates with audiences across generations.
